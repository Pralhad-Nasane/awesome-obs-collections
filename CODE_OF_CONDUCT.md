# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Welcoming newcomers and different viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that threatens the well-being of our community

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainer via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pralhadnasane).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Project maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter of an incident.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when representing the project in public spaces.
